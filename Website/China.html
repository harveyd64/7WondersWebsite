This massive ancient wall, made from cement, rocks and bricks is said to be viewable from space. To build it, materials were taken from nearby areas. It is the longest piece of human engineering at a length of almost 22000km. The height on average is about 6 to 7 metres. The Great Wall is located in the Huairo District in China. It has 7000 watchtowers for soldiers. Located in 16 different provinces, the Great Wall takes up a massive area in China, although it is most commonly visitied from Beijing. The first wall was built in around 700 BC, but the most famous one (Qin Shai Hong) was built between 226-200 BC. There have actually been 19 different walls known as The Great Wall, but the current one was finished in 1878.
