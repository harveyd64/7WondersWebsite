The beautiful mausoleum honours the dead and has a largely recognisable white dome at the top. Four smaller domes surround the large one in the centre. The entirity of it is made from white marble and it is 171 metres tall. It is based in the city Agra, which is in the Uttar Pradesh region. Geographically, this is in Northen India. It is considered to be one of India's most famous landmarks. Construction began in 1632, and the Taj Mahal was completed in 1653. It was built for Mumtaz Mahal as a final resting place - she was the third wife of the Mughal emperor (Shah Jahan).
